Tile-IQ

Privacy Policy

📋 Overview

Tile-IQ ("we", "our", or "the app") is a score tracking application for 101 Okey and Classic Okey card games. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, why, and how it is handled.

This application is developed and maintained by an individual developer. For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at mothenox46@gmail.com.

📦 Data We Collect

Tile-IQ does not require account registration and does not store any data on external servers.

All game data (player names, scores, game history) is stored locally on your device only using SQLite and SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

The following data may be collected by third-party SDKs integrated in the app:

  • Google AdMob — displays advertisements. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID), device information, and approximate location to serve relevant ads. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing — processes in-app subscription purchases. Payment and billing information is handled entirely by Google Play and is governed by Google Play Terms of Service. We do not receive or store any payment details.

🔒 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs (no Firebase Analytics, no Crashlytics).
  • We do not share any data with third parties beyond AdMob and Google Play Billing.
  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or location.

📱 Local Storage

Tile-IQ stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Game history and scores (SQLite database)
  • App preferences such as sound settings and language selection (SharedPreferences)
  • Subscription status — whether ads have been removed (SharedPreferences)

This data is only accessible to the app and is deleted when you uninstall the application.

💳 In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Tile-IQ offers an optional monthly subscription ("Remove Ads") processed through Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store any payment or billing information. All transactions are handled securely by Google.

Subscriptions can be managed or cancelled at any time through your Google Play account settings.

📢 Advertising

The free version of Tile-IQ displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use cookies and similar technologies to serve personalised ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalised advertising in your device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

Users who purchase the "Remove Ads" subscription will not see any advertisements.

👶 Children's Privacy

Tile-IQ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

🌍 International Users & GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectify, or erase personal data. Since we do not collect personal data directly, these rights primarily apply to data collected by Google AdMob and Google Play. Please refer to Google's privacy controls for exercising these rights.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
