👋 About Tile-IQ

Tile-IQ is a smart score tracker for tile games. It supports three game modes — 101 Okey, Classic Okey, and Rummikub — with automatic scoring, full game history, 14 achievements, and PDF export. The app is available on the APP STORE and GOOGLE PLAY.

No account or sign-in is required. All your data stays on your device.

🚀 Getting Started

Adding players

Open the app and tap New Game. Enter the names of the players who will participate. Player names are stored locally and can be reused for future games.

Starting a game

  1. Tap New Game on the home screen.
  2. Choose a game mode: 101 Okey, Classic Okey, or Rummikub.
  3. Add 2–4 players (depending on the mode).
  4. Tap Start to begin scoring rounds.

🎮 Game Modes

101 Okey

A round-by-round mode where each player accumulates points from the tiles left in their hand. The player with the lowest total score wins the game.

Classic Okey

A traditional Okey scoring mode where players track wins and losses across rounds. The last player standing takes the game.

Rummikub

Standard Rummikub scoring: at the end of each round, players earn or lose points based on remaining tiles. The player with the highest total wins.

✏️ Editing Rounds & Scores

Made a mistake entering a score? You can fix it at any time:

  1. Tap on the round you want to edit in the score table.
  2. Update the values for the affected players.
  3. Tap Save — totals are recalculated automatically.

You can also delete a round entirely by long-pressing it and choosing Delete Round.

🏆 Achievements

Tile-IQ includes 14 achievements that unlock as you play. Open the Achievements screen from the main menu to see your progress. Achievements are stored locally and progress is preserved between games.

📚 Game History

Every completed game is saved automatically to your local history. From the History screen you can:

  • Filter games by mode (101 Okey, Classic Okey, Rummikub)
  • Filter by player name
  • Re-open any past game to view its full scorecard
  • Delete individual games you no longer need

History is stored locally on your device and is removed if you uninstall the app.

📄 Exporting & Sharing PDF

You can export any scorecard as a PDF document:

  1. Open the game you want to export from History.
  2. Tap the Share or Export icon.
  3. Choose Save PDF to Photos or Share PDF to send it via Messages, Mail, or another app.

The first time you save to your photo library, iOS will ask for permission. You can grant or revoke this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos.

💳 Remove Ads Subscription

What it includes

The optional Remove Ads subscription removes all advertisements from the app. It is an auto-renewing monthly subscription processed by Apple In-App Purchase on iOS or Google Play Billing on Android. Tile-IQ does not see or store any payment details.

How to subscribe

  1. Open the app and go to Settings → Remove Ads.
  2. Tap the subscription button and confirm with Face ID, Touch ID, or your passcode.
  3. Ads are removed immediately after the purchase completes.

Restoring on a new device

If you reinstall the app or set up a new device, open Settings → Remove Ads and tap Restore Purchase. As long as you are signed in with the same Apple ID (or Google account on Android) used for the original purchase, your subscription will be restored at no extra charge.

Managing or cancelling

  • iOS: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Tile-IQ
  • Android: Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions

Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You will keep ad-free access until then.

🔒 Privacy & Data

Tile-IQ does not require an account and does not collect personal information. All player names, scores, and game history are stored locally on your device. For full details see our Privacy Policy.

🔧 Troubleshooting

The app crashes or freezes

Force-quit the app and re-open it. If the problem persists, restart your device. If it still happens, please contact us with the device model and iOS/Android version so we can investigate.

Subscription is not recognised

Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account that made the original purchase, then tap Restore Purchase in Settings. Allow up to a minute for the App Store / Play Store to respond.

PDF won't save to gallery

Check that the app has permission to add to your photo library: Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → Tile-IQ and grant Add Photos Only access.

Ads still showing after subscribing

Tap Restore Purchase in Settings. If ads continue, fully close and re-open the app.
